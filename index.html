Pinterest Mobile Analytics

BUSINESS STRATEGY • IA • LAYOUT

Businesses are highly valued content creators on Pinterest. Yet the tools given to them were not only clunky and difficult to use, but only existed on the web. I was tasked with adapting the web based analytics tools to the Pinterest mobile app. My work was informed by international quantitative and qualitative research to create a simplified yet powerful end to end experience. The new experience empowers businesses to get the most out of their Pinterest presence, create more content with greater confidence, and invest more readily in advertising.

Home Feed Algorithm Tuner

ETHICAL DESIGN • USER RESEARCH • USER ENGAGEMENT

Pinterest shows users an average of X number of images a day. Most of those images are picked by an algorithm that specifically tailors each choice to the user. But what happens when it picks wrong? What happens when the images are irrelevant, or even harmful? Over the course of a year, I created a suite of mechanisms that allows users on Pinterest to supply negative feedback on content and take control of their algorithm. This led to a X reduction in negative reviews relating to bad content and increased user engagement.

Building and Maintaining Design Systems

PUBLIC ENGAGEMENT • SYSTEMS • LEADERSHIP

For a little over a year, I was the main point of contact (with backup from several engineers) for the Pinterest Design System. I learned so much over the course of my time spent empowering a rapidly expanding team of designers. About Me

I’m a designer with experience in everything from print design and identity to product design and research. I held my own as an intern at Manual in 2015 and went on to be head of my graduating class at the University of Washington in 2016 with a job offer from Pinterest.

While at Pinterest, I drove the expansion and use of the Pinterest Design System that empowered a rapidly expanding team of designers to efficiently create and build experiences across the platform. I systematically clarified every major surface of both the web and mobile application, reducing clutter and driving growth in comprehension, engagement, and revenue. Later I led the charge to give users control over the mysterious and often incorrect algorithm deciding what content was shown to them. After that, I worked on the Business Analytics team to help businesses understand their performance on Pinterest and drive them to actively engage in our content ecosystem.

Over the past year, I have been travelling the globe with my partner around South America and the US. We had plans to travel to many more places but were thwarted by COVID. Although the year wasn’t what I planned, I am still thankful for the places and cultures I was able to see, and incredibly greatful for the time to center myself and spend time with family and friends.

Now I am turning my mind towards activism, mental health, social impact, and climate change. I am actively looking for my next job. If you are interested in talking with me, please don’t hesitate to email me at jvzingarelli@gmail.com .
